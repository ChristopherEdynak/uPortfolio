About Me

Born in 1976, Christopher Edynak is a professional full-stack web developer that was educated at Columbia University's Bootcamp. He grew up with a fascination with electronics and gadgets. Christopher was introduced to computers when he was just 3 years old.

Christopher moved to NYC in 2006 where he has had several careers. He started with his own Real Estate firm that closed during the crash of 2008. In 2009 Christopher begain a 6 year career as a chef. He currently handles all tech for a family operated business.
